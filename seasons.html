Discover the Seasons of Birds in Nepal

Spring

Spring brings new life to Nepal with various birds returning for the warmer months. Explore the species that migrate here during spring.

Summer

Summer in Nepal is hot and lively, with many birds thriving in the warmth and abundance of food. Discover the summer species here.

Autumn

Autumn is a beautiful transition into cooler weather, and various birds prepare for migration or stay in Nepal. Learn about the autumn species.

Winter

Winter in Nepal is chilly, and some birds migrate from colder regions while others stay to face the cold. Discover the winter birds here.

Monsoon

The monsoon season in Nepal brings heavy rains and lush greenery. Many birds thrive during this period, and others migrate to escape the rains.

Migratory Birds

Bird migration is the seasonal movement of birds between breeding and wintering grounds. It's usually from north to south or south to north.
