Discover the Birds of Summer

  1. Name: Indian Pitta

    A colorful bird with a distinct call, often seen in forests and scrublands.

  2. Name: Asian Koel

    Famous for its melodious call, often heard during summer.

  3. Name: Common Hawk-Cuckoo

    Also known as the "brainfever bird," active in woodlands.

  4. Name: Black Drongo

    A fearless bird often seen perched on wires and trees.

  5. Name: Eurasian Golden Oriole

    A bright yellow bird found in forests and open lands.

  6. Name: Chestnut-headed Bee-eater

    A beautifully colored bird that feeds on flying insects.

  7. Name: Blue-throated Barbet

    Recognized for its vibrant green and blue plumage.

  8. Name: White-throated Kingfisher

    A common sight near rivers and wetlands.

  9. Name: Greater Racket-tailed Drongo

    Noted for its long, rackety tail and mimicry skills.

  10. Name: Common Myna

    An adaptive bird found in urban and rural areas.

  11. Name: Oriental Magpie-Robin

    A small bird with a lovely song, found in gardens and forests.

  12. Name: Scarlet Minivet

    A strikingly colored bird in forested regions.

  13. Name: Plumbeous Water Redstart

    Found near fast-flowing rivers and streams.

  14. Name: Jungle Babbler

    Social birds often seen in noisy flocks.

  15. Name: Asian Paradise Flycatcher

    A graceful bird with long tail feathers, especially males.
